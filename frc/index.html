Full Moon - Selûne's illuminated side is facing Toril. Selûne appears to be completely illuminated by direct sunlight.
Waning Gibbous - Selûne appears to be more than one-half but not fully illuminated by direct sunlight. The fraction of Selûne's disk that is illuminated is decreasing.
Last Quarter - One-half of Selûne appears to be illuminated by direct sunlight. The fraction of Selûne's disk that is illuminated is decreasing.
Waning Crescent - Selûne appears to be partly but less than one-half illuminated by direct sunlight. The fraction of Selûne 's disk that is illuminated is decreasing.
New Moon - Selûne's unilluminated side is facing Toril. Selûne is not visible (except during a solar eclipse).
Waxing Crescent - Selûne appears to be partly but less than one-half illuminated by direct sunlight. The fraction of the Selûne 's disk that is illuminated is increasing.
First Quarter - One-half of Selûne appears to be illuminated by direct sunlight. The fraction of Selûne 's disk that is illuminated is increasing.
Waxing Gibbous - Selûne appears to be more than one-half but not fully illuminated by direct sunlight. The fraction of Selûne 's disk that is illuminated is increasing.
